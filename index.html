Bio

Welcome to my website, My name is Lee, and I am a passionate self-taught Developer.

My interest in coding started while I was running my business in 2007. I was an onsite manager of my clothing outlet. I faced unaffordable Electronic Point of Sale (EPOS) software that was poorly made and did not suit my needs. So I set about with only free time determination and an internet connection, to make my own. If i had known how difficult a task lay ahead, i might never have started. But I had a lot of free time and eventually, after a few years, I had a working solution.

So began my life long love of software development and problem-solving.
